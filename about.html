Design-Centered Product

We provide leadership consulting and mentorship to grow product people, empower product teams, and put the experience of your ultimate customer at the center of your product design and development workflow.

Design in Product founder Christian Crumlish has been leading product and UX teams for more than two decades.

He’s helped large companies and startups for consumer and enterprise while providing platforms and generative tools for collaboration and creativity.

He’s helped teams build everything from gesture-based native mobile cloud apps to compassionate social environments designed to bring out the best in human nature for companies like PathCheck.org, DataCoral.co, CityInnovate.com, NESThealth.io, EduWorks, 7cups.com, CloudOn (acquired by Dropbox), AIM, AOL, Yahoo, Extractable, Grateful Dead Productions, Rhino Records, Visa, Sprint, Radio Free Blogistan, and Enterzone.

Christian provides leadership consulting and mentorship to grow product people, empower product teams, and put the experience of your ultimate customer at the center of your product design and development workflow.
